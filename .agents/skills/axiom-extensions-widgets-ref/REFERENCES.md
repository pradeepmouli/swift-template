# Extensions & Widgets References

## WWDC Sessions

### iOS 18+ (Latest Features)
- **WWDC 2025-278** — What's new in widgets: Liquid Glass rendering, CarPlay widgets, axiom-visionOS support, Relevance configuration

### iOS 18 (WWDC 2024)
- **WWDC 2024-10157** — Extend your app's controls across the system: ControlWidget, Button, Toggle, Value Providers
- **WWDC 2024-10068** — Bring your Live Activity to Apple Watch: supplementalActivityFamilies, activityFamily environment
- **WWDC 2024-10098** — Design Live Activities for Apple Watch: Smart Stack design, glanceability, compact view

### iOS 17 (WWDC 2023)
- **WWDC 2023-10028** — Bring widgets to life: Interactive widgets, App Intents, animations
- **WWDC 2023-10194** — Design dynamic Live Activities: Lock Screen, StandBy, Dynamic Island, design principles

### iOS 16 (WWDC 2022)
- **WWDC 2022-10184** — Meet ActivityKit: Live Activities basics, ActivityAttributes, starting/ending
- **WWDC 2022-10185** — Update Live Activities with push notifications
